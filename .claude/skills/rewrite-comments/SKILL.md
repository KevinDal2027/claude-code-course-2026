Rewrite the comments in the file $0 based on the level of understanding of $1 you have of the file. Rate your understanding on a scale of 1-5, where 1 means you have no understanding and 5 means you have a complete understanding. Then, rewrite the comments in the file to reflect your current level of understanding.
